TIRED LANDLORDS

READY TO WALK AWAY
FROM YOUR RENTAL?

Being a landlord sounds great in theory — until you're dealing with late rent, costly repairs, difficult tenants, and the never-ending demands of property management. What started as an investment can quickly turn into a second job you never wanted.

Selling a rental property through traditional channels comes with its own headaches. Most buyers don't want to deal with existing tenants, and you may feel stuck waiting for leases to expire or going through a lengthy eviction process before you can even list. That's time and money you don't have to spend.

The Summit Group buys rental properties with tenants in place — no evictions needed. We purchase the property as-is, handle the transition, and close on your timeline. You walk away clean, with cash in hand, and without the burden of being a landlord for one more day.
